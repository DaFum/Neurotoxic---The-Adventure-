## 2024-04-01 - [Add focus-visible to Pause and Game Menu Buttons]
**Learning:** React Three Fiber projects with complex UI overlays (like `UI.tsx` and `Game.tsx`) heavily rely on keyboard navigation for accessibility, especially when the mouse might be bound to a canvas or joystick controls. Many critical menu buttons lacked clear focus indicators.
**Action:** Always add `focus:outline-none focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-offset-2 focus-visible:ring-offset-black` to buttons in game overlays to ensure they are keyboard accessible and visually distinct when focused without relying on hover states.
